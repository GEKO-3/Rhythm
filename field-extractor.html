Step 4: Check Form Settings

Make sure your Google Form is configured correctly:

  1. Go to your form editing page
  2. Click the Settings tab (gear icon)
  3. Under "Responses" make sure "Accepting responses" is ON
  4. Check if you have any restrictions (like requiring sign-in)
