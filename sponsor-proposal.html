ABOUT RHYTHM BODUBERU

Rhythm Boduberu is the premier traditional Maldivian Boduberu music group, dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Maldives through authentic performances. Our group consists of talented musicians and performers who are passionate about keeping the traditional art of Boduberu alive for future generations.


We perform at cultural events, festivals, resorts, and private functions, bringing the authentic sound and energy of traditional Maldivian music to audiences both locally and internationally. Our mission is to showcase the beauty of Maldivian culture while creating memorable experiences through our captivating performances.

OUR YEARLY PLAN

Join us in our journey to promote traditional Maldivian culture throughout the year with our comprehensive performance and outreach program.

New Drums

Our current drums have seen better days, and we aim to acquire new, high-quality instruments to enhance our performances.

Studio Upgrade

Invest in upgrading our recording studio to produce high-quality audio and video content that showcases the Boduberu.

Recording Projects

Produce a series of audio songs that highlight our unique sound and style.

Video Song

Produce and release Boduberu video recordings to document and share our cultural heritage and stories with the world.

Academy

Establish an academy to teach and promote traditional Boduberu music to the next generation.

Brand Outreach

Expand our online presence to reach larger audiences and share the beauty of traditional Maldivian music.
